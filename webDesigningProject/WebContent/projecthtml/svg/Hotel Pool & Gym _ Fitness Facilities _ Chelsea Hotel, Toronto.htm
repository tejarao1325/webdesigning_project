    Adult Pool & Fitness Centre

    For those wanting to relax, Deck 27’s indoor pools offer a hidden urban oasis of quiet and calm. The Fitness Centre includes separate male and female saunas for post-workout relaxation. These facilities have a seasonal outdoor sundeck for warm summer afternoons and evenings.

    Stay active

    Swim laps year-round in the heated pool or work up a sweat with our full cardio-challenging fitness centre. Deck 27's facilities will meet the most active person's wellness needs.

Our Location

Directions

Hello Hotel Toronto is conveniently located in the northwest Toronto, adjacent to the Humber River and is accessible via taxi or transit from the city's major international airport.

From Pearson International Airport

By Car

Pearson Airport is approximately 8,5 km and 15 minutes west of the hotel. Airport limousine services and taxis are located on the ground level of Terminal 1 and the arrivals level at Terminal 3. Fares average approximately C$20.

TTC

Buses between Hello Hotel Toronto and Pearson Airport take 35 minutes and run every 15 minutes.
